Branches

Just like how there is a team of people within a game design unit, there are different teams within a game design company. The company can be split into:

  • Company Owners (Financial, Sales, Sponsors)
  • Designers (Deciding gameplay, blueprint)
  • Programmers (Apply computer programming aspect, code)
  • Artists (visual aspects, attractive appeal for the game)

Similar to a company these teams are further separated into specific roles and includes a type of ranking system among the different branches and individuals.

Examples of big game companies include Blizzard and Activision.
